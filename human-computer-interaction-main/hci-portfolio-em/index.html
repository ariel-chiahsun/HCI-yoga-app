Project Overview

Y360 is an augmented reality (AR) yoga app designed to provide immersive 360° video courses, offering new experiences for yoga practitioners. With the recent pandemic, the way people learn yoga has changed dramatically, resulting in an increasing number of individuals opting for home-based yoga practice instead of attending physical classes. Y360 aims to address the challenges of remote yoga learning by introducing an interactive learning environment that offers personalized training courses using action-recognition systems.
The main contributions of the project are summarized as follows:

  • Created an AR interface that utilizes 360° video technology to simulate the feeling of attending physical yoga classes
  • Integrated action-recognition technology to provide real-time feedback and improve users' posture and technique
  • Created personalized training courses that adapt to the user's skill level and meet their individual needs

Research

User Interview

To gain insights into our target users and address key pain points, we conducted semi-structured interviews with five students from National Taiwan University who had practiced yoga consistently for over a year. Our focus was on understanding their experiences, needs, and expectations of yoga.

We found out that......

  • Verbal guidance was as effective as hands-on guidance for learning yoga
  • Real-time feedback was desired to bring attention to body mechanics during practice
  • Personalized learning experiences and ownership of practice space were expected
  • The yoga studio was viewed as a place for learning and mutual support among students

Competitive Analysis

After conducting a thorough analysis of competitors, we identified two main areas that require improvement: personalized courses and a holistic view of yoga positions. Specifically, we looked into two types of competitors: yoga apps and virtual reality yoga games. One of the limitations we found with current products is that they offer limited courses and restrict user choices. Additionally, most yoga app videos only provide side and front views of instructors demonstrating poses.

To differentiate ourselves, our objectives are to

  • Provide both pre-designed and customized courses, allowing users to plan their workouts
  • Integrate AR technology to create an immersive experience, where users can explore yoga poses further through rotating and zooming 360° interactive videos
  • Enhance the real-time feedback system, allowing users to reflect on their performance and improve in future classes

Develop and Design

To create a learning environment that addresses these user needs, we have developed four product design guidelines:

  • Provide clear and sufficient demonstrations with verbal instructions
  • Offer real-time feedback to correct errors and improve performance
  • Allow users to create custom yoga sequences based on their preferences
  • Create a platform for learners to share their experiences and connect with others

Low-Fidelity Prototype Design

After conducting user research and competitive analysis, we prioritized four key sections for our product: Explore, My Courses, My Profile, and Community. These sections address the needs of our target users and provide a better user experience (UX). Using sketching techniques, we designed the UX based on the guidelines we proposed for creating an ideal learning environment for yoga practitioners.

High-Fidelity Prototype Design

To add personality to our product and connect with users, we used UX writing to craft the text copies that appear throughout the interface of our prototype.

Flexible and Customizable Yoga Journey from the Start

Our onboarding process allows users to set preferences for content and training levels, and provides the option to set daily reminders. This approach creates a customizable and flexible yoga journey from the outset.

Discover a World of Yoga Possibilities with Our Explore Feature

Our Explore feature offers users the opportunity to explore the latest yoga trends and discover additional courses to complement their journey. With search and advanced filtering features, users can easily find courses that suit their preferences, including level, focus, space, and positions.

Elevate Your Yoga Practice with Interactive Course Videos

Our Course Videos offer step-by-step audio and visual instructions, allowing users to optimize their poses through real-time visual and audio feedback. With the ability to compare their posture to the optimal position, users can reduce the risk of injury caused by incorrect postures. Our 360° instructional library of poses provides a comprehensive view from multiple perspectives, giving users the ability to master their skills.

Personalized Learning and Community Building: My Courses and My Profile Features

In the My Courses section, users can curate their own personalized yoga training by building a library of preferred videos. They can set reminders and track their progress with ease, using our platform. Our user profiles and feed feature enable direct connections with other learners, fostering an environment for sharing experiences and peer feedback.
